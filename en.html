Sobre mí

Hi. It is no coincidence you got here... You are seeking to return to your home, to your customs, to your essence, to yourself. How far away do you feel from yourself? How long have you've been gone? Or... How long have you been lost?

I've asked myself this question countless times, and it's there when the first thing I tend to do is to take a deep breath and try to feel as a part of this Universe; and then, thank life for the good and the bad. Everything has left me a teaching.

You might be wondering, who am I and how do I work.

First, I'd like to tell you that I'm an eternal seeker, that I always searched and tried to find the reasons, what cannot be understood by reason. I always wanted to find what leads us beyond the limited.

And from this inward research, I trained myself in Psychoanalysis, then turned to the cognitive, to conclude in an integrative approach, where the prime focus is not about learnt theory, but about what the consultant - patient needs.

I studied grief, terminal disease, addiction, and anxiety disorders; but over the years and the acquired experience, I've tried to make the patient achieves more than just symptomatic relief; my intention is set, so that he/she can reach its soul.

The way to do it is through words and conscious relaxation, through music and images. I also use Biodecoding, Mindfulness techniques and the Alkýmica self-healing method.

I invite you to you contact me so that we can meet in a video interview via Zoom, to shorten the distances that separate you and what you left behind.
